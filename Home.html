Kavala Prasad Foundation

Kavala Prasad Foundation is a humanitarian organization dedicated to improving the lives of underprivileged children, families, and communities. Our focus lies in creating sustainable solutions that ensure access to education, healthcare, clean drinking water, and livelihood opportunities.

Our Purpose & Vision

We believe in building a society where every individual thrives with dignity and equal rights. Through impactful initiatives, awareness programs, and community development efforts, we aim to bring lasting positive change and empower the next generation for a brighter tomorrow.

The Best Way to Make a Difference in the Lives of Others

The Kavala Prasad Foundation

The Kavala Prasad Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to eradicating classroom hunger by providing mid-day meals to children in need. We aim to ensure that no child goes hungry during school hours.

Since 2005, we have collaborated with schools, local communities, and volunteers to build sustainable programs that support education, nutrition, and long-term well-being.

Our Mission

To empower communities with education, nutrition, healthcare, and social support — ensuring equal opportunities for all.

Our Vision

To create a future where every child receives proper nutrition, education, and the chance to build a brighter, healthier life.
